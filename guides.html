The Guides of Your Faire

Meet Our Dedicated Team

Our guides have been part of The PNW RenFaire since the very beginning, helping shape the experience and welcoming guests from the very first day. Knowledgeable, approachable, and deeply familiar with every corner of the grounds, they’re here to make your visit smooth, fun, and memorable. Whether you’re searching for a favorite performer, the perfect vendor booth, or a quiet place to take it all in, our guides are always ready to help.


More than anything, our guides love sharing the faire with others. They take pride in showing guests around, answering questions, and helping everyone feel at home in our little corner of the Renaissance world. Their passion, professionalism, and genuine enthusiasm are part of what makes The PNW RenFaire such a welcoming place to explore.

The Lively Merchant

Sophie has been a part of The PNW RenFaire since the beginning. As one of the founding members, she has ensured that all of our core values are upheld from guests to vendors.

Sophie has called this area home for the past 20 years and loves to make guests welcome regardless of where they come from. She specializes in knowing the ins-and-outs of the vendor area and is the best guide to show you where to find the deals.

The Dashing Ranger

Clark has been with the Faire since the beginning. As one of the original members, he’s helped shape the adventurous spirit of the faire and brings a steady, watchful presence to the grounds.

Having called the PNW home for many years, Clark loves guiding guests along the paths less traveled and pointing out the hidden gems of the faire. With a ranger’s keen eye and a guide’s experience, he’s always ready to help you find great shows, unique vendors, and the perfect spots to explore.

The Poised Princess

Isabelle has been part of The PNW RenFaire for many years and is one of its longtime members. She brings grace, warmth, and a touch of royal charm to every corner of the grounds.

Having spent years in the PNW, Isabelle delights in welcoming guests as if they were visiting her own court. She loves guiding visitors through the faire with elegance and kindness, pointing out must-see attractions, favorite vendors, and the little moments of magic that make the day feel truly special.

The Prophetic Bard

Frank has been part of The PNW RenFaire for many years and is one of its original members. He’s been filling the faire with music, stories, and laughter for as long as most guests can remember.

A lifelong PNW local, Frank can often be found with his lute in hand, sharing songs while helping guests find their way. With a bard’s heart and a guide’s experience, he’s always happy to point you toward great performances, lively spots, and the perfect place to stop and enjoy the music.
