How We Got Started

Our Story Starts with You

At Rensissance Faires across the country, we would experience a wide variety of communities. Each community is different and we would return home to the PNW and miss what made this area so special. We realized it then - what we were missing was our sense of home. This began our journey to find what made this part of the country so inviting and how we could encompass that through the joy of the renaissance faire.


The PNW is known for being... well, quirky! Portland, where the concept for The PNW RenFaire was born, is full of unique visual arts and colorful inviduals. We knew that the goofy people of the PNW deserved a RenFaire that felt like home to them. We promise to bring you the quirky, accepting, and passionate warmth you known from the Portland community to our faire.


We are excited for you to join us! Come make our faire yours at The PNW RenFaire.

Core Values

What sets us apart from every other renaissance faire in this country? It's simple. Our values. Here at PNW RenFaire, we hold our community to a high standard. Every person, be them a vendor or an entertainer, is held to our core values. This means that everyone is treated with the kindness and respect they deserve.

Don't believe us? Come to one of our events and see what makes us a step above the rest.

Community

Community is at the heart of everything we do at The PNW RenFaire. From performers and vendors to guests and volunteers, we believe a faire is more than an event—it’s a shared experience built on connection, kindness, and belonging. We strive to create a welcoming space where everyone feels seen, celebrated, and invited to be part of the story.

Entertaiment

Our entertainment brings the spirit of the Renaissance to life through music, performance, and playful storytelling. From lively acts to quiet moments of wonder, each performer helps create an atmosphere of joy, imagination, and shared delight that invites guests to fully step into the magic of the faire.

Passions

Our vendors are skilled artisans, makers, and merchants who bring creativity and craftsmanship to every corner of the faire. Each booth tells a story, offering thoughtfully made goods and interactive experiences that celebrate tradition, artistry, and the joy of supporting small creators.

Vendors

The PNW RenFaire is guided by values of inclusivity, respect, and authenticity. We are committed to creating a space where everyone feels welcome to express themselves, explore freely, and connect with others in a spirit of openness and mutual appreciation.
